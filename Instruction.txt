Instructions

1. Install AutoHotkey and choose ANSI 32 bit
2. Open CS:GO
3. Run the script
4. Enjoy
5. Turn Numlock off in order use the controls. All controls are on the Numpad.
On first run, press Numpad5 to hide the text, then Numpad2 and then Numpad5 again to bring up the menu.
6. Use 8 and 2 to go up and down the menu, and 4 and 6 to turn on/off
